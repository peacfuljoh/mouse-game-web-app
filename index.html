Mouse

Welcome to Mouse

In this game, you are a hungry rodent on a quest for queso. Pesky cats are patrolling the area as usual. Luckily, you're strong and can push blocks around to trap the cats and turn them into cheese. Yum!

But beware. As you progress through the levels, the cats will get faster, smarter, and stronger. You don't have to trap all of them to move on, but cheese is a must.

Use the W, A, S, and D keys to move around. And the R key to restart a level if you mess up.

Good luck!


Cheaters can use the "-" and "+" keys to go forward and backward through the levels. Also, you can press P to freeze the cats. Don't be a cheater!
